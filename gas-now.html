Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are ETH gas fees right now?

ETH gas fees are transaction fees that you pay when interacting with the Ethereum network. They vary depending on network congestion and transaction complexity.

What are crypto gas fees?

Crypto gas fees are the costs required to conduct transactions or interact with smart contracts on blockchain networks. These fees are paid to miners or validators who process transactions.

What is the current Gwei price?

Gwei is a unit of measurement for gas fees on the Ethereum network. It represents a fraction of 1 ETH and is used to price transactions and smart contract executions.

Why do Gwei prices fluctuate?

Gwei prices fluctuate based on the network's demand. When more people use the network, the Gwei price increases due to higher competition for block space.
