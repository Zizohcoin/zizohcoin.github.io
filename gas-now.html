Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are gas fees?

Gas fees are transaction fees required to complete transactions on a blockchain. They vary depending on the network's traffic and the complexity of the transaction.

Why do gas fees vary?

Gas fees fluctuate based on the supply and demand of the blockchain network. When more people are transacting, the gas fees tend to rise.

How can I reduce my gas fees?

You can reduce gas fees by making transactions during less busy times, optimizing your transactions, or using Layer 2 solutions like Polygon.
